SAMDF / DPA Powers (Part VIA)

s72P: Identity and Purpose

Power: Request full name, residential address, and reason for presence.
Applies to: Any person in or near a Defence Practice Area (DPA).
Threshold: Reasonable belief that person is about to commit/has committed an offence against Part VIA.

s72R: Search of Persons/Vehicles

Power: Search person, clothing, and any vehicle under their control.
Threshold: Necessary for the purpose of safety or security of the DPA.
NOTE: If person refuses search, they may be prohibited from entering or ordered to leave the DPA immediately (s72S).

s89A Crimes Act: Arrest of Trespassers

Power: Arrest without warrant any person found trespassing on Cth land.
Duty: Deliver to a "Constable" (NT Police) as soon as practicable.
